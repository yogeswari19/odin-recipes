Mango Lassi

A mini vacation in a glass!

Description

Mango lassi is very rich and luxurious drink with yogurt and mangoes.
It is the perfect summer drink when served chilled.


Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups mangosmangos - peeled, seeded, chopped, and chilled
  • 1 ½ cups plain yogurt
  • ½ cup cold milk
  • 2 tablespoons heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons confectioners' sugar
  • ½ teaspoon ground cardamom

Directions

  1. Combine mangos, yogurt, milk, cream, confectioners' sugar, and cardamom in a blender
  2. Blend until smooth and frothy. Pour into glasses and serve immediately.
